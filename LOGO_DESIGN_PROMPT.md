# Logo Design Prompt for Post Duplicator Plugin

## Design Brief

Create a modern, professional logo for "Post Duplicator," a WordPress plugin that allows users to duplicate posts, pages, and custom post types with one click.

## Core Concept

The logo should visually represent the act of **duplication** or **copying** while maintaining a clean, professional appearance suitable for a WordPress plugin. The design should convey efficiency, simplicity, and reliability.

## Visual Elements to Consider

### Primary Concepts

- **Duplication/Copying**: Two identical or overlapping shapes, documents, or posts
- **Cloning**: Mirrored or stacked elements
- **Efficiency**: Clean, streamlined design suggesting speed and ease
- **WordPress Integration**: Subtle nod to WordPress (optional, not required)

### Design Styles

- **Modern & Minimalist**: Clean lines, simple shapes, contemporary feel
- **Professional**: Suitable for a business/developer tool
- **Friendly**: Approachable, not intimidating
- **Scalable**: Works well at small sizes (favicon) and large sizes (header)

## Specific Design Directions

### Option 1: Document Duplication

**Description**: Two overlapping or stacked document/page icons, with one slightly offset to show duplication. Could include a small "copy" indicator (like a plus sign, arrow, or duplication symbol).

**Visual Elements**:

- Two rectangular documents/pages
- One document slightly offset or behind the other
- Optional: Small duplication symbol (two overlapping circles, copy icon, or arrow)
- Clean, flat design style

**Color Suggestions**:

- Primary: Blue (#2271b1 - WordPress blue) or modern gradient
- Accent: Green or orange for the duplication indicator
- Background: Transparent or white

### Option 2: Circular Duplication Symbol

**Description**: Two overlapping circles or a circular icon with duplication elements. Could incorporate a document/page silhouette within the circle.

**Visual Elements**:

- Circular badge/icon shape
- Two overlapping circles or mirrored elements
- Optional: Small document icon inside
- Modern, geometric design

**Color Suggestions**:

- Gradient from blue to purple or blue to teal
- Single color with depth/shadow
- Clean, professional palette

### Option 3: Stacked Posts Icon

**Description**: Two or three rectangular blocks (representing posts) stacked with a slight offset, creating a "stack" effect that suggests duplication.

**Visual Elements**:

- 2-3 rectangular blocks/posts
- Slight rotation or offset to show depth
- Optional: Small "copy" badge or indicator
- Flat or slightly dimensional design

**Color Suggestions**:

- Blue tones with gradient
- Monochrome with accent color
- WordPress-inspired color scheme

### Option 4: Abstract Duplication Symbol

**Description**: An abstract symbol representing duplication - could be two connected shapes, a mirrored design, or a symbol with a duplication indicator.

**Visual Elements**:

- Abstract geometric shapes
- Symmetry or mirroring
- Modern, minimalist approach
- Unique, memorable symbol

**Color Suggestions**:

- Bold, modern colors
- Gradient effects
- Professional color palette

## Technical Requirements

### Dimensions & Formats

- **Primary Logo**: Square format (512x512px minimum, 1024x1024px recommended)
- **Icon Version**: Square icon (256x256px) for plugin directory
- **Favicon**: 32x32px and 16x16px versions
- **Formats**: SVG (vector), PNG (with transparency), and possibly AI/PSD source files

### Scalability

- Must be recognizable at 16x16px (favicon size)
- Must look professional at 512x512px (plugin directory)
- Should work in both light and dark backgrounds (consider providing two versions)

### Style Guidelines

- **Style**: Modern, clean, professional
- **Complexity**: Medium - detailed enough to be interesting, simple enough to scale down
- **Typography**: If including text, use a modern, readable sans-serif font (logo should work with or without text)
- **Colors**: Professional palette, WordPress-friendly (blues, greens, or modern gradients)

## Color Palette Suggestions

### Option A: WordPress-Inspired

- Primary Blue: #2271b1 (WordPress blue)
- Accent: #00a0d2 (WordPress light blue)
- Success Green: #46b450 (for duplication indicator)
- Background: Transparent or white

### Option B: Modern Gradient

- Gradient: Blue (#2271b1) to Purple (#7c3aed) or Teal (#14b8a6)
- Accent: White or light color for contrast
- Background: Transparent

### Option C: Professional Monochrome

- Primary: Dark blue or dark gray (#1e293b or #0f172a)
- Accent: Bright blue or green (#3b82f6 or #10b981)
- Background: Transparent

## Mood & Tone

- **Professional**: Suitable for developers and content managers
- **Modern**: Contemporary design that doesn't feel dated
- **Trustworthy**: Clean, reliable appearance
- **Efficient**: Suggests speed and ease of use
- **Approachable**: Not intimidating or overly technical

## What to Avoid

- ❌ Overly complex designs that don't scale well
- ❌ Cliché copy/paste icons (unless done in a unique way)
- ❌ Dated design styles (skeuomorphism, heavy gradients from 2010s)
- ❌ Too many colors or busy compositions
- ❌ Designs that don't work at small sizes
- ❌ Generic document icons without unique elements

## Reference Inspiration

Consider these design directions:

- **WordPress Plugin Icons**: Clean, modern, professional
- **Developer Tools**: GitHub, VS Code, etc. - professional but approachable
- **Duplication Concepts**: Look at how other tools represent copying/duplication
- **Modern App Icons**: iOS/Android app icons - clean, recognizable, scalable

## Final Deliverables Needed

1. **Main Logo**: Full logo with text "Post Duplicator" (horizontal layout)
2. **Icon Only**: Square icon without text (for plugin directory)
3. **Favicon**: Small versions (16x16, 32x32)
4. **Dark Background Version**: If using light colors, provide a version for dark backgrounds
5. **Source Files**: Vector format (SVG, AI, or EPS) for future edits
